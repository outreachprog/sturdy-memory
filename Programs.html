Program

Handog Karunungan: Pagsulong sa Kinabukasan will provide Educational Sessions to enhance the learning experience of the students, particularly in areas where they may need additional support. The sessions aim to:

  • Enhance Basic Skills:
    -Literacy and Language: Improve reading comprehension, vocabulary, and understanding of the Filipino language through storytelling and interactive games.

    Mathematics: Strengthen basic math skills such as addition and subtraction in a fun and engaging way through games like number hopscotch and quick math quizzes.
  • Promote Active Learning:
    -Provide hands-on activities that encourage students to participate, ask questions, and practice their skills in a dynamic environment. This helps in reinforcing concepts and making learning enjoyable.
  • Build Confidence:
    By involving students in activities where they can succeed and have fun (such as completing a word puzzle or solving math problems), the sessions aim to boost their confidence in their academic abilities.
  • Foster Critical Thinking and Problem-Solving:
    The activities are designed to help students think critically, solve simple problems, and make connections between what they are learning and real-world scenarios.
  • Encourage Collaboration and Interaction:
    Group activities and games encourage teamwork and communication among the students, promoting social and cooperative learning.
